Things that orbit Earth

If you live, for example, in Seattle, space is closer to you than the ocean.

In fact the largest project humanity accomplished, International Space Station, is circling just 400km above us, which is about the distance from New York to Boston.

Humanity sent a lot of things up in space, and most of them are around our planet.

Earth's Orbits

Space starts at Karman line - an official boundary of space, 100km up.
